About Us

We are a team of students who developed the Smart Calendar & Scheduling System to help people manage their time better. Our goal is to make planning events, setting reminders, and organizing tasks simple and useful for everyone—especially students and employees.

Our Project

This project was built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It includes useful features like a calendar, timeline, and notifications. We also added an admin page that lets users manage content by adding, editing, or deleting tasks and events.

  • Calendar & Tasks

    The calendar lets users add events, set times, and keep track of upcoming tasks in a clear and visual way.

  • Notifications & Reminders

    The system shows alerts and reminders to help users stay on track with their schedule.

  • Timeline View

    A timeline helps users see all tasks and events sorted by time, making it easier to plan ahead.

What We Offer

Our system is made for users who want a simple way to organize their day. Whether it’s for school, work, or personal use, our features help you plan and stay on schedule.

  • Event Scheduling

    Quickly add, view, and manage your daily events using the built-in calendar.

  • Reminders & Alerts

    Get automatic reminders for important tasks and never miss a deadline.

  • Timeline View

    View all your upcoming activities in order to better manage your time.
